## ğŸ”„ How Approval Works

Your requests are reviewed by your supervisor, who considers:

1. **Your Seniority** (how long you've worked here)
   - More senior = higher priority
   
2. **Department Limits**
   - Only 1 person per department can take vacation the same week
   - Different departments can overlap

3. **Priority Order**
   - Your Week #1 is considered before Week #2, and so on

**A:** You can submit a new request, but the old one will be replaced.

### Q: Why did I get yellow/red warnings?
**A:** 
- ğŸŸ¡ **Yellow** = Someone more senior wants the same week (you might still get it)
